Development Journal

Honest notes from building a living town. What shipped, what's next, what broke.

JULY 2026

The Town Learns to Share

Chests went server-side this month: one shared world where a looted chest is gone for everyone until the respawner picks it back, and two players tapping the same chest race for it — exactly one wins. NPCs also found their voices: talk to Martena and she'll actually answer you, in her own words.

Also new: professions with daily work, nine trainable skills, a magick cave that only appears to the observant, and a proper map you can tap to travel.

EARLIER

Foundations

The world is data — every building, road, and NPC route lives in editable files, and the in-game editor writes them back. Models swap without moving a single placement. NPCs walk their rounds computed from the clock itself, so every visitor sees the same town without a server ticking.
