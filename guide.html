New Player Guide

So you've wandered into Emberhollow. Here's how not to embarrass yourself in front of the Watch.

Getting Around

Tap or click the ground to walk there. Drag to spin the camera a full circle, pinch or scroll to zoom. The compass (top right) always knows which way is north; the map beneath it (M) shows the whole town — tap anywhere on it to travel.

Your Character

Your portrait sits top-left — tap it (or C) for your character sheet: reputation, gold, and your strongest skills. The K panel tracks all nine skills, which grow from doing, not grinding menus: walk and Endurance rises, open chests and Perception sharpens, talk to townsfolk and Speech-Craft loosens.

Find Work

Three townsfolk are hiring. Martena the Baker takes apprentices, Watchman Hale swears in recruits, and Captain Brammel by the eastern shore always needs another line in the water. Tap a townsperson, then tap Become a… — one trade at a time, and switching means leaving the old life behind.

Daily Tasks

Every profession has a daily task (the pill, bottom-left): the Watch runs timed rounds of the watch-points, bakers gather flour and well-water for the morning bake, and fishers chase the day's fish — the tide picks the same species for everyone. Tasks pay gold and town reputation, and reset at midnight.

Chests

Chests hold coin, tools, and stranger things. Once looted, a chest vanishes — and every few minutes, one missing chest somewhere in town restocks. When the notice appears, the hunt is on.

Cooking

Raw fish is a choice; cooked fish is a meal. Stand near the tavern hearth or a campfire, open your inventory (I), and Use a raw fish. Fair warning: new cooks burn a third of what they touch. It gets better. The gulls disagree, but it gets better.

The Cave

Sometimes, beyond the fields, there is a cave. Sometimes there is not. Those with sharp eyes see it more often than those without. If you find it, go inside. That's all anyone will say.
